Games!

A game is a structured from of play,usually undertaken for entertainment or fun,and sometimes used as an educational tool.Many games are also considered to be work oe art. Games are sometimes played purely for enjoyment,sometimes for achievement or reward as well.

Ludwig Wittgenstein was probably the first academic philosopher to adress the definition of the word game.in his Philosophical Investigation,Wittgenstein argued that the elememts of games,such as play,rules,and competition, all fail to adequately define what games are. from this Wittgenstein conclued that people apply the term game to a range of disparate human activities thet bear to one another only what one might call family resemblances. As the following game definitions show this conclusion was not a final one and today many philosophers like Thomas Hurka think Wittgenstein was wrong and that Bernard Suits definition is a good answer to the problem.
